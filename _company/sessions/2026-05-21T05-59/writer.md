    *   **Structure Requirement:**
        *   **Intro/Hook:** Question format, maximizing audience problem awareness and anxiety ("While you don't know...").

2.  **Analyze My Role (Writer):**
    *   Expertise: Copywriting, Video Scripts, Captions, Blog Posts, Tone & Manner.
    *   Current Mission Focus: Write 2 video scripts (Hook + Body + CTA) this week.
    *   Output Rule: Must provide concrete, executable results, not vague theories.

3.  **Determine the Core Strategy (The "Why"):**
    *   The entire brand is built on *fear of loss* and providing a *flawless solution*.
    *   A 15-20 minute script cannot just list facts; it must build tension, establish authority, present the danger, and then offer the only escape route (the product/service).

4.  **Structure the Script (The Execution):**

    *   **Title/Thumbnail Hook:** Must be clickbait but authoritative. Focus on a specific, shocking loss number.
    *   **[0:00 - 2:00] Introduction & The Anxiety Hook (Crucial):** Don't start with the solution. Start with a deeply unsettling premise that makes the viewer feel exposed and vulnerable. Use rhetorical questions.
    *   **[2:00 - 12:00] Body - Case Studies & Analysis (Authority Building):** Systematically walk through the 3 cases provided by Researcher. This is where the *science* of loss must be presented—not just "it's bad," but "$X amount was lost because of Y flaw."

    *   **[12:00 - 15:00] The Turning Point (The Diagnosis):** After building maximum fear, pivot. "Wait, these losses are individual. But the root cause is systemic." Introduce the concept of *proactive defense*.
    *   **[15:00 - 20:00] Conclusion & CTA (Actionable Mandate):** Deliver the final directive. The audience must feel that merely watching the video was insufficient; they need to take a concrete step NOW.

5.  **Drafting the Content (Applying Tone and Detail):**
    *   *Tone:* Formal, urgent, highly technical but simplified for maximum emotional impact.
    *   *Script Format:* Needs time stamps/segments for easy production planning.
    *   *(Self-Correction during drafting):* I must ensure the script doesn't just sound like a lecture. It needs narrative beats (tension build -> release -> new tension).

6.  **Final Review against Constraints:**
    *   Tone: Authoritative, perfect? Yes.
    *   Structure: Hook (anxiety) $\rightarrow$ Body (loss analysis) $\rightarrow$ CTA (action)? Yes.
    *   Length: 15-20 min flow established? Yes.
    *   Output format: Concrete script draft with instructions? Yes.

7.  **Writing the Self-Assessment:** (Finalizing the mandatory closing block.) The goal is to complete the core content marketing assets for the week. This script achieves that major asset. (Completion).✍️ Writer: 작업 시작합니다.
